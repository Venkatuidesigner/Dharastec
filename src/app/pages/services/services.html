Our Process

At Dharstec Solutions, we follow a structured yet flexible process to ensure every project delivers measurable value to our clients. From the first conversation to post-deployment support, we stay focused on understanding your business and delivering solutions that fit perfectly.

1

Consultation

We begin by understanding your business challenges, goals and existing systems. Our team collaborates closely with you to identify key opportunities for digital solutions.

2

Defining the Scope

Based on our discussions, we prepare a detailed scope document outlining objectives, deliverables, timelines and estimated budget. This ensures mutual clarity and alignment before development begins.

3

Solution Development

Our development team designs and builds tailored web, mobile or cloud-based solutions, ensuring scalability, performance and user-friendliness at every stage.

4

Demonstration & Feedback

We provide live demos at key milestones so you can visualize progress and share feedback. This iterative approach ensures the final product meets your expectations perfectly.

5

Implementation

Once approved, the solution is deployed and integrated into your operational environment. Our experts ensure a seamless transition with minimal disruption to your business.

6

Continuous Support

After implementation, we continue to assist with maintenance, upgrades and performance optimization ensuring your solution evolves with your business needs.

Engagement Models

At Dharstec Solutions, we understand that every business has unique requirements, timelines and budgets. That’s why we offer flexible engagement models that let you choose how you want to collaborate with us, whether you need full project ownership or dedicated development resources.

Hourly Engagement

Hire our developers or consultants on an hourly basis for complete flexibility. - Ideal for short-term projects, urgent tasks, or ongoing support where scope evolves over time.

  • ✔️ Best for: Short-term assignments, urgent fixes or projects with evolving scope.
  • ✔️ Flexibility: Scale your team up or down at any time based on workload.
  • ✔️ Benefit: Pay only for the actual hours worked, ensuring cost efficiency.

Monthly Engagement

Build your extended team with our monthly engagement model. - Perfect for businesses that require dedicated resources but prefer to manage tasks and priorities directly.

  • ✔️ Best for: Businesses seeking dedicated resources for continuous development or support.
  • ✔️ Flexibility: Hire individual experts or full teams who work exclusively on your projects.
  • ✔️ Benefit: Enjoy predictable monthly costs and consistent productivity.

Fixed Project Outsourcing

Outsource your project to us for end-to-end execution at a fixed cost. - We take full ownership of the project from requirement analysis to delivery ensuring you get reliable outcomes within defined timelines.

  • ✔️ Best for: Well-defined projects with clear requirements, timelines, and deliverables.
  • ✔️ Flexibility: Our team manages the entire lifecycle from analysis to deployment.
  • ✔️ Benefit: Guaranteed outcomes within agreed budget and schedule.
